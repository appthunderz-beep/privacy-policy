Thunderz ⚡ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

1. Information We Collect

Thunderz does not collect any personal information. All your tasks, routines, and focus data are stored locally on your device through SharedPreferences and never uploaded to any server.

2. Permissions Used

  • Do Not Disturb (DND): To enable Focus Mode.
  • Usage Access: To detect app usage for Focus Mode warnings.
  • Overlay Permission: To display focus alerts above other apps.
  • Notification Access: To manage distraction control.

These permissions are used only while using Focus Mode and are not shared or stored externally.

3. Data Security

Your data (tasks, notes, and focus stats) is securely stored in local app storage. Deleting the app removes all data permanently.

4. Third-Party Services

Thunderz does not integrate with or share data with any third-party APIs or services.

5. Children’s Privacy

Thunderz is suitable for all ages (3+) and does not collect any information from children.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please reach out to us at appthunderz@gmail.com.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Thunderz after changes means you accept the revised terms.
